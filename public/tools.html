Our Custom Tools

Discover our suite of free interactive tools created to enhance your D&D adventures and connect with The Crimson Court universe.

Dice Roller

Roll any combination of dice with our custom dice roller. Perfect for quick checks or complex rolls during your D&D sessions.

Features:

  • Roll multiple dice types simultaneously
  • Add custom modifiers to your rolls
  • Save and reuse common dice combinations
  • View your roll history
  • Beautiful animations and sound effects

Character Quiz

Discover which Crimson Court heir you're most like! Take our personality quiz to find your royal match and learn about your leadership style.

Features:

  • 15 thought-provoking scenario questions
  • Detailed character match analysis
  • Personalized result card with character art
  • Downloadable results to share
Take the Quiz

House Quiz

Which noble house of Ederia would you belong to? Discover your house allegiance, from the fierce Ravencrest to the cunning Foxhaven.

Features:

  • Discover your noble house alignment
  • Learn about house values and history
  • Receive your personalized house crest
  • Download your house membership card
  • Compare your results with friends
Find Your House

Interactive Map

Explore the kingdom of Ederia and the broader world of Illica. Discover the secrets of each noble house and the rich lore of this fantasy realm.

Features:

  • Interactive zoomable fantasy map
  • Detailed information on regions and cities
  • Political boundaries and noble domains
  • Points of interest with historical notes
  • Toggleable map layers and search function
Explore the Map

Party Loot App

Party Loot is a free web application designed by a fellow D&D enthusiast to streamline your party's treasure and inventory management.

Features:

  • Maintain precise records of your party's wealth across all denominations, with automatic currency conversion
  • Generate view-only links for party members, allowing them to view the inventory without needing an account
  • Locate items swiftly by filtering by owner, type, or search terms
Manage Your Loot

4-Pillar World Builder

Create cohesive D&D campaign settings using the 4-Pillar framework. Build your world by defining the rules for magic, religion, society, and wilderness.

Features:

  • Step-by-step world-building process
  • Define fundamental rules across four pillars
  • Create conflicts, barriers, and tier progression
  • Add inverse twists for complexity
  • Export your world as document or text
Build Your World
